Intro

TPO6-15作为25年9-10月份对托福题目的初见，整理完毕已经是11月的第二个周了，虽然只做了阅读和听力，且题目过时，但其难度是显而易见的，非常的细节，逻辑严谨，题材广泛。除去词汇量的不足，感觉上刷题越多越有题感（越能蒙对）下面是Reading Section和Listening Section的反思，或者说错题本罢。

Reading

TPO6

1.Which of the sentences below best expresses the essential information in the highlighted sentence in the passage? Incorrect choices change the meaning in important ways or leave out essential information.

Only the last of these was suited at all to the continuous operating of machines, and although waterpower abounded in Lancashire and Scotland and ran grain mills as well as textile mills, it had one great disadvantage: streams flowed where nature intended them to, and water-driven factories had to be located on their banks, whether or not the location was desirable for other reasons.

第一句确定了主系表的结构，主语"last of these"对应了前一句话的"running water"，省略掉 "although"和"whether"引导的从句，即得出核心大意。

4.The word “exploited” in the passage is closest in meaning to ()

A. utilized

B. recognized

C. examined

D. fully understood

"exploit"译为“利用;开发;发挥”，"utilize"译为“使用;利用;应用”

结合上下文来看，本段将介绍一种新的能源。第二段之后便开始介绍蒸汽机的起源，“early in the century"也对应了"had long been known”

7.The phrase “grew accustomed to” in the passage is closest in meaning to ()

A. began to prefer

B. wanted to have

C. became used to

D. insisted on

"accustomed"译为“习惯的;适应了的”

10.The Industrial Revolution would not have been possible without a new source of power that was efficient, movable, and continuously available.An introductory sentence for a brief summary of the passage is provided below …

第一段：在蒸汽机等发明前只有三种"sources of power"，其中"running water"适合机器持续运转但仍有“季节性”的缺点。

第二段：”大气压机“的原理，及浪费能源和效率低的不足之处，之后是对它原理的改进。

第三段：瓦特蒸汽机，导致了"possible deeper and deeper mining", “new form of nighttime illumination”, “motive force of the Industrial Revolution”

第四段：蒸汽机在19世纪的扩大使用，主要讨论了交通方面，水运的局限以及解决运输问题的火车的发明。

本题的三个错误选项，我认为是与文章主题的偏差问题，即并没反映题干

A: This “atmospheric engine,” invented by Thomas Savery and vastly improved by his partner, Thomas Newcomen …

D,E: 和"The Industrial Revolution would not have been possible without a new source of power that was efficient, movable, and continuously available."关系不大，即和蒸汽机关系不大，D依据第三段，E依据第四段第一句。

A. nonmaterial

经济学的词汇不足问题，"deposit"表示钱的时候，有“存款；预付款，定金；押金”的意思，"deduct"为“减，减去；扣除”，"owe"为“欠债；该偿还”，"balance"为“结存，结余；差额”

雇员问学生今天有没有带押金(deposit)，学生说带了，说他同学都知道之后还得再付押金之外的更多的钱，雇员说我明天算好把差额(balance)告诉你。
